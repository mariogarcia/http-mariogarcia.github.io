Propositional logic: Contrapositive

Just a quick note about the contrapositive is that it’s very useful to prove some statement. For example:

All drivers in Madrid, drive a bike
— original statement

Now if we can prove that the contrapositive is true, then we can prove that the original statement was wrong.

All non-bike drivers, live outside Madrid
— contrapositive

Only proving that at leat one non-bike driver lives in Madrid will prove the initial statement to be wrong.
